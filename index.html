The concept of Timecaps is simple and elegant

TimeCaps is a free video app that allows you to create a time capsule, a video message that you send to any of your phone contacts to be unlocked on a date decided by you.

01

TimeCaps is a new type of social media app. It build excitement and anticipation around the unlocking of a future moment, rather than giving users instant gratification. You can send messages to one person, a group or to yourself.

02

When you send a video time capsule, the recipient sees a notification with a single image from the video. He or she will wonder what you have said in your message but will have to wait - from five minutes to 50 years!

The possibilities of the content you send are endless - from heartfelt messages to predictions and motivational messages. It's fun. It's meaningful. It's uplifting.
